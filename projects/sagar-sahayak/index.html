üß† Core Engineering Contributions#

1. Integrated AI Pipeline#

I engineered the bridge between two distinct AI domains to create a seamless flow:

  • Enhancement: Utilized a U-Net deep learning architecture to remove water haze and perform natural color correction.
  • Detection: Integrated YOLOv8 to automatically identify submarines, divers, and marine life with high confidence scores.
  • Real-time Performance: Optimized the pipeline to achieve 15-30 FPS, critical for live underwater surveillance.

2. Quantitative Quality Validation#

To move beyond subjective judgment, I implemented a scientific metrics engine:

  • PSNR & SSIM: Industry-standard measures to ensure structural similarity to ground truth.
  • UIQM: A specialized Underwater Image Quality Measure to validate the effectiveness of the AI enhancement.
  • Impact: Achieved up to 88% improvement in PSNR on specialized datasets.

3. Security & Mission Control#

Designed the system for defense-grade reliability:

  • Access Control: Implemented a secure authentication system with password hashing.
  • Edge Optimization: Investigated model quantization techniques to allow the system to run on resource-constrained AUVs and ROVs.
