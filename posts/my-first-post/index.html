My First Blog - An Introduction

October 2, 20253 min read

Hello Readers, This is my first ever attempt of writing a Blog so there might be some grammatical errors and I also don’t know the basics of blogging so I apologise in the beginning. I don’t have some very crazy stories to write here in my blogs(or maybe I have;) but the motive of me starting this blog is to certify my skills and record my journey of growth 📈

Before going on any further let me tell about myself, If you might not know yet my name is Aditya Chooramani, I am currently in my second year of college pursuing BCA(Bachelors in Computer Applications). I am very fond of computers and technology and this was my motivation for pursuing this degree and moreover i like cybersecurity very much because firstly it as a kid I wanted to be a hacker but growing up my dream also got a word added, which is, “Ethical” cause i don’t wanna end up in jail and secondly the security industry is on BOOM. I know AI is very much in the IT space right now but someone has to secure the slop created by AI 🤖

Recently I also picked up a new hobby of electronics, I got an Arduino UNO R3 and have been tinkering with circuits for some time. I am passionate about ethical hacking, pentesting, red and blue teaming etc. and I am continuously learning new things everyday 🔁

I also know some part of python, C language and basic website building and all those regular stuff any other software engineer nowadays are able to do but the thing is I don’t want to be the ‘any other’ engineer, I want to become a full stack engineer(not developer), I have the vision to be the person with the set of skills to do code, build stuff and also secure it, which I definitely know is NOT EASY AT ALL but atleat I will try my hardest to reach as close to my goals as possible ⚙️

I have took part in some hackathons that took place in my college, I created a security project which got in 4th position out of 35 teams and I plan to scale in some time and I am also a good speaker and am proficient in Hindi and English. I also would like to point that I use Linux by the way, lol that is one step closer to becoming a security professional and I also love to play around with other computers and I have also created my own NAS Server 📶

I guess this is enough for the first blog, I would try my best to post atleast one blog per week even if no one reads it but it would be my trail which will be left when I am leaving behind in the path of growing up and achieving success. Before signing off let me clarify all of this is NOT generated by AI and I have wrote it myself, I tried to be as professional I could be for the first blog and would also try harder to write more efficient and on point blogs from the next time ✍️

In the next blog i would like to tell you about my workflow of how I learn, what are my tools and some other things I have learned or built 🛠️

Signing off
